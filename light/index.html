A LITTLE MORE ABOUT ME

Since 2003 I’ve been practicing yoga as a way to relieve stress. My first yoga class was on a VHS tape in my living room (Yes, VHS!). In the moments to follow that first session I felt this overwhelming peace, and since then I’ve been hooked. Many years later, I’ve had the opportunity to attend a handful of teacher trainings by some of the most powerful and profound teachers in the industry. I love to share this beautiful practice with my students.

Through connecting the physical practice of yoga with the breath I believe you can achieve incredible things physically, spiritually and emotionally. It’s not only a great way to cross train for any sport, it is also key in finding peace in your day to day life.
